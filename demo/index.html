Everything you need to know

UpneX agents are AI-native, not people. They work 24/7 without sick days, vacation, or turnover risk. A VA takes 2–4 hours to process a screening; our agent does it in 30 seconds. And every output is audit-gated before it reaches a tenant or owner — so you get speed without sacrificing accuracy.
All applicant data is processed on SOC 2-compliant infrastructure. We never store sensitive SSN or credit data — it passes through to your existing screening vendor (TransUnion, Experian, etc.). Your data stays in your systems.
Every output is audit-gated — a human reviews the AI's decision before it's sent to a tenant or owner. You get the speed of AI with the safety of human oversight. We also maintain a full audit trail of every decision for compliance purposes.
48-hour onboarding. We connect to your existing PMS (AppFolio, Buildium, Yardi, or others), map your workflows to our agents, and go live. No new software to learn, no migration, no downtime.
