The Challenge

You have been selected from a competitive pool of applicants to take part in a live, paid selection challenge. Your task is straightforward in its goal — and demanding in its execution:

Design, build, and deploy an autonomous lead generation agent for a growing accounting firm. The agent must identify potential clients, qualify them against a defined profile, and deliver a pipeline of actionable leads — with as little manual intervention as possible.

How you get there is entirely up to you. The strategy, the tools, the channels, the architecture — all of it is your call. We are not looking for a specific approach. We are looking for the person who can think clearly, execute independently, and build something that actually works.

What You're Solving For

The client is an accounting firm looking to grow its client base. Their ideal clients are small and medium-sized businesses that need ongoing accounting, bookkeeping, or tax services.

Your agent should change that. At the end of five days, the firm should have a live, running system — not a proposal, not a slide deck, not a prototype — a working agent actively generating leads.

What to Submit

  • 01 A live, running lead generation agent (demo must work on Day 5)
  • 02 A leads output file — structured list of leads generated, with source, contact info, and your qualification notes
  • 03 A brief write-up (1–2 pages) explaining your approach: what the agent does, how it works, and why you made the choices you did
  • 04 A handover guide — enough documentation for someone else to run and maintain the agent

How the Winner Is Selected

Submissions will be evaluated across four dimensions. Every competitor is scored on all four. The highest total score wins.

Dimension Weight What We're Looking At
Lead Quality
30%
What share of submitted leads genuinely fit the target client profile? Accurate qualification beats raw volume every time.
Lead Volume
25%
Total number of qualified leads generated within the 5-day window. More is better — but only if the quality holds.
System Autonomy
25%
How much does the agent run on its own? A system that needs constant babysitting scores low. A system that runs while you sleep scores high.
Clarity & Handover
20%
Can you explain what you built and why? Is the documentation clear enough that someone else could take over and run it?

In the event of a tie on total score, Lead Quality is the tiebreaker. A smaller list of well-qualified leads will always outrank a large list of noise.

The Internship

The winner will be offered a paid internship with upneX Solutions and LS Properties — the two organisations behind this challenge. This is not a ceremonial title. You will be working on real systems, real clients, and real business problems from day one.

The internship is structured with long-term prospects for the right person. If you demonstrate the ability to think independently, ship working systems, and grow with the work — there is a path beyond the internship.

We are a small, execution-focused team. We value people who figure things out, document what they build, and take ownership of outcomes.

Ground Rules

Allowed: Any tools, APIs, platforms, AI models, automation frameworks, or programming languages. Open-source libraries, paid tools (at your own cost), cloud services — all fair game.

Not allowed: Purchasing or using pre-compiled lead lists. Spamming, scraping platforms that explicitly prohibit it in their ToS, or any practice that could create legal or reputational exposure for the client.

Confidentiality: The client's name and business details are shared with participants only. Do not publish, share, or discuss the client's identity outside this competition.

Submission deadline: All deliverables must be submitted before the Day 5 presentation slot. Late submissions will not be accepted.

Originality: All work must be your own. You may use AI tools to assist you, but you must be able to explain every part of your system in the live walkthrough.
