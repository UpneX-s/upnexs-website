Our AI Employees

Eight Agents. One Back Office. Zero Overhead.

Each agent is built for a single operational role. You can start with one and add more as your portfolio grows.

🔍

Screening Agent

Qualifies applicants end-to-end — so your team only sees move-in-ready tenants.

  • Reviews applications & documents
  • Requests missing items from applicants
  • Scores & routes to your decision criteria
🔄

Renewal Agent

Starts renewal outreach 90 days out. Increases retention without your team lifting a finger.

  • Contacts tenants at 90/60/30-day marks
  • Handles negotiation within your parameters
  • Updates lease records in your PMS
🔧

Maintenance Agent

Every maintenance request handled, tracked, and resolved — without clogging your inbox.

  • Logs & categorizes requests 24/7
  • Dispatches your approved vendors
  • Follows up on completion & tenant satisfaction
🏠

Leasing Agent

Responds to every inquiry, books tours, and chases applications so no lead goes cold.

  • Responds to Zillow, MLS, direct inquiries
  • Books showing tours & sends confirmations
  • Chases application completion follow-ups
📋

Onboarding Agent

New tenant move-in processed start to finish — docs, portals, key pickup, first impressions.

  • Collects docs & sets up payment portals
  • Sends move-in instructions & checklists
  • Coordinates utility setup & access codes
📊

Owner Attrition Agent

Tracks portfolio health and keeps owners informed before they start asking questions.

  • Flags at-risk owners before they leave
  • Sends proactive monthly performance reports
  • Answers owner questions from your data
📍

Vacancy Agent

Monitors days-on-market and takes action when listings stall — before vacancies cost you owners.

  • Syndicates listings to Zillow, MLS, + more
  • Flags stale listings for price review
  • Reports vacancy trends weekly
📰

Newsletter Agent

Keeps your owner relationships warm with zero manual effort — branded, data-driven monthly updates.

  • Auto-generates monthly owner newsletters
  • Includes maintenance summary & market data
  • Branded to your company, sent on your schedule
